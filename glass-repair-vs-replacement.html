Glass Repair vs Replacement: When to Repair Instead of Replace

When faced with damaged glass, many homeowners and business owners automatically assume they need a complete replacement. However, in most cases, professional glass repair is not only possible but often the better choice. At OhMyGlass.ca, we specialize in determining when repair is the optimal solution, saving you time, money, and unnecessary disruption.

Why Glass Repair is Often the Better Choice

Professional glass repair offers significant advantages over replacement in many situations. It's typically 60-80% less expensive than full replacement, can be completed in hours rather than days, and preserves your existing window frames and hardware. Most importantly, modern repair techniques can restore glass to near-original condition for most types of damage.

Types of Damage That Can Be Repaired

Our expert technicians can successfully repair various types of glass damage, including small cracks, chips, scratches, and even some larger cracks. We use advanced techniques like resin injection, glass polishing, and specialized adhesives to restore structural integrity and visual clarity. Even foggy double-pane windows can often be repaired by replacing just the sealed unit rather than the entire window.

When Replacement is Actually Necessary

While repair is preferred in most cases, there are situations where replacement is the only viable option. This includes extensive damage affecting more than 30% of the glass surface, damage to the window frame itself, or when the glass is part of an outdated system that no longer meets safety or energy efficiency standards. Our professionals will always provide an honest assessment of your options.

The Cost-Benefit Analysis

Glass repair typically costs $100-300 for most residential applications, while replacement can range from $500-2000 or more depending on the window type and size. Additionally, repair work can usually be completed the same day, minimizing disruption to your home or business. This makes repair an attractive option for budget-conscious property owners who want to maintain their property's value without the expense of full replacement.

Professional Assessment is Key

The key to making the right decision is getting a professional assessment. Our experienced technicians can evaluate the extent of damage, assess the structural integrity, and determine whether repair or replacement is the best long-term solution. We'll provide you with a detailed explanation of your options and their respective costs and benefits.
